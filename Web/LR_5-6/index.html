"Petersburg cuisine" is a unique gastronomic phenomenon that arose as a result of the fusion of two gastronomic cultures: traditional Russian cuisine and elegant French cuisine with some additions of other national culinary recipes. The Petersburg Cuisine project, launched in 2014, is aimed at reviving the culinary traditions of St. Petersburg and promoting our city not only as a cultural, but also as the gastronomic capital of Russia.
